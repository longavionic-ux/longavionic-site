TANK-4000

Tank Floor Leakage Flux / Eddy Current Video Inspection System

Tank-4000ME tank bottom inspection system is designed to check and measure corrosion and thickness decrease of tank bottom plate. The system improves the complete inspection process with industrial touch integrated computer, reliable and stable performance, high sensitivity and high efficiency. The structure is optimized with embedded system module, six-wheeled design and new arm structure.

  • Real-time Signal Acquisition & Analysis
  • Applicable to Carbon Steel & Stainless Steel
  • Automated Inspection Report Generation
  • Top/Bottom Surface Defect Identification
  • Laser Defect Positioning
  • High-Definition Video Capture
  • A-Scan / C-Scan Imaging
  • Tank Floor Mapping
