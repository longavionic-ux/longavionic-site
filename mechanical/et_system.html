PEUT-913

EDDY CURRENT ARRAY / Phased Array Ultrasonic Integrated Testing Instrument

PEUT-913 Eddy Current Array/Phased Array Ultrasonic Integrated Tester breaks the limitations of traditional NDT by combining PAUT and ECA technologies in one revolutionary device. It delivers all-in-one detection of internal, surface, and near-surface flaws without coating removal, providing real-time 2D/3D C-scan imaging and automatic defect sizing. The system adapts to complex components like welds and turbine disks using electronic scanning and custom wedges. Its software correlates data from both techniques to reduce false calls, while full digital traceability meets aerospace and nuclear quality standards.
