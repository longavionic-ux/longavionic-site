STAR-5000

Request Quote
Portable Synthesis Tester for Aviation Radio

The STAR-5000 is a portable synthesis tester used for field functional testing of airplane communication, navigation and surveillance systems.

It can be used to inspect aircraft ILS, ATC, MB, RA, ADF, VOR, DME and other systems or equipment.

It integrates more than a dozen CNS test functions into a software-defined device, significantly reducing the quantity, volume and cost of test equipment.

STAR-5000 can be powered by a removable Li-ion battery, supporting more than 3 hours of continuous operation.

It also supports 28V DC or 220V AC power supply for flexible usage scenarios.

Equipped with a touch screen, the system allows detailed signal configuration for comprehensive functional testing.

Functions

  • VOR simulator
  • ILS simulator
  • Marker beacon (MB) simulator
  • Transponder (ATCRBS / Mode S) tester
  • DME simulator and tester
  • VHF COMM tester
  • ADS-B simulator and tester
  • TCAS simulator and tester
