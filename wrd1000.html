WRD-1000
Weather Radar Test Set

The WRD-1000 simulates radar echoes and measures transmission power, frequency, sensitivity, and more of airborne WXR systems, enabling complete testing of these devices.

It is suitable for engineering development, verification, manufacturing, and maintenance testing.

The WRD-1000 is equipped with a touch-screen and user-friendly graphical software. It supports both local and remote operation and can function as a standalone instrument or be integrated into an ATE system.

It can replace the RDX7708.

Key Features

  • Simulates meteorological, turbulence, and other radar echoes
  • Adjustable echo pulse width and frequency offset
  • Automatically acquires and tracks radar transmissions
  • Measures carrier frequency
  • Measures PRF of radar transmissions
  • Measures peak power of radar pulses
  • Coupled output, detector output, and trigger interfaces
  • LAN remote control (UDP/IP supported)
  • Supports VXI-11 and SCPI command control
