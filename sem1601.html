SEM-1601

Radio Altimeter Simulator

SEM-1601 is an independent altitude simulator used in the field. It uses support rods to position antenna units under the aircraft’s altimeter antennas.

The antenna units connect to the SEM-1601, providing simulated altitude signals to the onboard altimeter.

  • Frequency: 4200MHz ~ 4400MHz
  • Adjustable attenuation: 10dB ~ 60dB
  • Altitude range: 0m ~ 10000m
  • Height modes: Pulse, continuous wave, pseudo-code ranging
  • Dimensions: < 207mm × 140mm × 91mm
  • Weight: < 2.2 kg
  • Working temperature: -40°C ~ +50°C
  • Storage: -40°C ~ +70°C
  • DC IN
  • Status LED
  • ON / OFF
  • RF Input
  • Reserved
  • RF Output
